a few words about us

Aux Vieux de l'Arcole is a charming establishment nestled in the heart of Paris, just a stone's throw away from the historic Notre-Dame Cathedral. This delightful café and restaurant exude an old-world charm that captures the essence of Parisian culture and tradition. As you step into Aux Vieux de l'Arcole, you are instantly transported to a bygone era. The interior is adorned with vintage décor, featuring rustic wooden furniture, antique photographs, and a warm, inviting atmosphere. The walls are lined with shelves showcasing old books, adding to the nostalgic ambiance that permeates the space.

The café is renowned for its delicious and authentic French cuisine. From the aroma of freshly baked croissants to the rich flavors of traditional coq au vin, the menu at Aux Vieux de l'Arcole offers a delightful selection of classic dishes. Whether you're indulging in a leisurely breakfast, savoring a decadent lunch, or enjoying a romantic dinner, each culinary creation is prepared with care and passion.

Offering the Best Pizza in Los Angeles

One of the highlights of this charming establishment is its picturesque location. Situated on Île de la Cité, a small island in the Seine River, Aux Vieux de l'Arcole offers breathtaking views of the surrounding historic architecture. Guests can enjoy their meal while admiring the enchanting beauty of the nearby Notre-Dame Cathedral or simply watch the world go by as locals and tourists alike traverse the quaint streets of Paris.

But it's not just the delectable food and captivating setting that make Aux Vieux de l'Arcole special; it's the people. The attentive and friendly staff, with their warm smiles and genuine hospitality, create an atmosphere that makes guests feel like they are part of an extended family. They are passionate about sharing their love for French cuisine and ensuring that every visitor has a memorable experience.

Providing Top-notch Customer Service

Whether you're a local Parisian searching for a cozy spot to unwind or a traveler yearning for an authentic taste of French culture, Aux Vieux de l'Arcole offers a delightful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. It embodies the timeless charm and elegance that has made Paris a beacon of romance and sophistication for centuries.

In addition to its cozy café and restaurant, Aux Vieux de l'Arcole also offers exceptional catering services that bring the essence of Parisian cuisine to any event or gathering. Their catering service is known for its attention to detail, culinary expertise, and commitment to delivering an unforgettable dining experience.

Integrity & Dedication

Whether it's a corporate event, wedding reception, or private party, Aux Vieux de l'Arcole's catering team works closely with clients to create a bespoke menu that suits their preferences and the occasion. From cocktail canapés to multi-course meals, each dish is crafted with precision and showcases the finest ingredients sourced from local suppliers

The catering service extends beyond the delectable food itself. The team at Aux Vieux de l'Arcole understands the importance of presentation and ensures that every detail, from the elegant table settings to the exquisite plating, is meticulously attended to. They create an atmosphere that is both inviting and sophisticated, leaving a lasting impression on guests.
